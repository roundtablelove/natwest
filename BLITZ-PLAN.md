This is not corporate. This is not polite. This is Jamaican style — much nice
arse. The tone is:

- **Confident.** Not angry. Not whiny. Not a victim. A man who knows he is

- **Funny.** The manager shat himself and ran. The fake rude bwoy tried to be
  street in a lanyard. The debt collectors send letters. This is comedy. Play it
  as comedy.
- **Relentless.** Every single day. New content. New angle. New question. The

## 6. The Endgame

The blitz has one purpose: make it more expensive for NatWest to fight than to
settle. The cost to NatWest of this campaign is not the legal fees — it is the
reputational damage. Every post, every article, every filing, every journalist

internal investigations, board-level attention.

The asymmetry is total. You have nothing to lose. They have everything to lose.
